Privacy Policy

Units Converter, developed by Azhar Zouhir, is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' data. Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads, and we use the Flutter framework to build and develop the app.

AdMob collects device information, location, and anonymous usage data to serve relevant ads to users. This data is used to personalize ads and improve the user experience. AdMob may also share this data with third-party partners for ad targeting and measurement purposes.

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information from our users. We only use anonymous data for the purposes of improving our app and delivering relevant ads to users.

Our app may collect crash reports to help us identify and fix issues with the app. This data is anonymous and does not include any personal information.

Users can opt-out of personalized advertising by going to the Google Ad settings page.

We may also collect user feedback and email addresses for the purpose of improving our app and providing support to users. We will not share or sell this information to third parties.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or the use of your personal information, you can contact us at azhar.zouhir.team@gmail.com.
